Fruits by definition are the fleshy or ripened dry parts of a plant that has seeds. Fruits develop in the flower of a plant, while vegetables develop on other parts of the plant. In many parts of the world, humans and animals depend on fruits as their main source of energy and food. Examples of fruits are apples, oranges, and watermelon.
